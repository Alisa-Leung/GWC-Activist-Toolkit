Digital Literacy

As we move between the real and digital world, let's combat misinformation and learn to be cognizant of our online interactions.

Learn More

What is it?

Digital literacy is the ability to interact with the online space while knowing how to stay safe. You're probably using that ability right now! Being able to navigate your way around the internet is an extremely useful skill, and it can be applied to various fields of interest.

So what?

It's always important to take steps towards our online safety. Whether this means keeping personal information private, thinking critically before acting, or being mindful of your interactions with others, there are many precautions you can take to stay safe online.

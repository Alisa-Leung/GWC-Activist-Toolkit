Google

Google: Be Internet Awesome has many fun resources directed at young children and families. These guides cover many tips and useful information for being safe and civil on the internet in a fun and digestible way.

NAMLE

The National Association for Media Literacy Education has many guides for educators, parents, and young digital citizens. They provide useful tips on how to be media literate or more aware of the information we consume.

FOSI

The Family Online Safety Institute conducts research on various topics concerning online safety and well-being. They also actively advocate for a better online environment through government policy.
